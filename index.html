Controls:
R to restart
Keyboard: Arrow keys to move, Space to use net, Z to glue bridges
Gamepad: Dpad or left analog to move, A to use net, B to glue bridges
Goal:
Catch as many butterflies as possible
Adding butterflies to your collection will give you glue. Bridges degrade over time, use glue to repair them.
If a platform isn't attached to the main platform it will fall. If you have less than 3 platforms you lose.
Rumor tells of a golden butterfly, perhaps catching it will bring good luck.
